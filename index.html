Direction Quest

The Lost Traveler — Learn to Give & Follow Directions

How to Play

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys, drag the on-screen joystick on phones, or just click/tap anywhere on the ground to walk there. Hold Shift (or push the joystick further) to run.
  • Talk: Walk up to a townsperson (glowing ring) and press E, tap the Talk button, or click/tap them directly.
  • Listen: Townspeople will give you directions in English. Read carefully!
  • Prove it: Answer a quick challenge about the directions you were given.
  • Travel: Follow the directions to the marked destination on your mini-map.
  • Journal: Press J anytime to see your quest log.
  • Pause: Press Esc to pause, save, or adjust volume.
